Layla and Boog's Puppies

Or Booger-nut, as my family calls me. I am almost five years old, and I am the toughest dog on the Merrill family farm. I am the proud papa of one litter of puppies so far, and I can't wait to be a papa of more and get them all to great forever homes.

At home, I love to go outside and run, maybe sometimes a little too much because my legs are so short. My mom, Mandy gives me the best rubs. I hope you talk to her when you are picking out one of my beautiful pups!

I'm a cutsie full of excitement, and jealousy. I hate it when Boog is getting the attention, so I worm myself into every situation. I also am a protective mama and bite Boog gently when he is bullying his big brother, the hooman, Wyatt.

At home, I get lots of love and rubs, and I always keep my eye on the prize. The prize is hands. Hands that give me the best rubs. My dad, Ryan loves to give me rubs.
